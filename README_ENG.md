# Russia Housing Market BI Dashboard

## Price Analysis, Cost Drivers & Regional Insights

---

## Project Overview

This project is a Power BI dashboard designed to analyze the Russian residential real estate market.

It provides insights into pricing structure, key cost drivers, and regional differences.

---

## Business Context

The real estate market is highly heterogeneous, with prices depending on multiple factors such as area, number of rooms, floor level, and region.

A data-driven tool is required to:

- Analyze market conditions  
- Identify price drivers  
- Compare regions  
- Understand market structure  

---

## Objectives

- Analyze the Russian real estate market  
- Identify key pricing drivers  
- Explore regional differences  
- Build an interactive dashboard  

---

## Analytical Approach

- Aggregated metrics (average price, price per m², listings count)  
- Data grouping (area, floors, rooms)  
- Filtering and segmentation  
- Outlier detection and handling  

---

## Key Insights

- **Average property price is around 5.7M RUB, with price per m² at ~95-110K RUB.**

- **1–2 room apartments account for over 60% of all listings**, indicating a mass-market focus.

- **Price per m² decreases as total area increases**, showing a typical scale effect.

- **Regional price differences exceed 2-3x**, highlighting strong geographic inequality.

- **Top-15 regions account for the majority of listings**, indicating market concentration.

- **Floor level and building type affect price, but are secondary factors.**

- **Data anomalies were identified**, including zero prices, extreme values, and listings outside Russia.
